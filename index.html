💡 Kerala Diet Tips

  • Cook with minimal coconut oil (measure with a spoon, don't pour).
  • Replace Matta rice portions partially with veggies like Thoran or Mezhukkupuratti to stay full.
  • Avoid deep-fried snacks (Pazhampori, Vada) and bakery items.
  • Drink warm Jeera water throughout the day for better digestion.
  • Ensure every meal has a protein source (Fish, Chicken, Mutta, Kadala, or Cherupayar).
